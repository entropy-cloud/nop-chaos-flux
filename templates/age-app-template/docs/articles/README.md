# Articles Index

## Purpose

`docs/articles/` holds outward-facing explanatory writing about the methodology behind this template.

These articles are not the operational source of truth for day-to-day execution. Their job is to help new readers understand why the template is shaped this way.

## Read These First

- `from-spec-driven-development-to-attractor-guided-engineering.md` - practical comparison between AGE and spec-driven workflows for business application teams
- `attractor-before-harness-ai-large-scale-development-methodology.md` - methodology article explaining why stable convergence structure must come before harnesses and guardrails

## Notes

- Keep these articles explanatory and reader-friendly.
- Do not move mandatory operating rules here; those belong in `AGENTS.md` or `docs/context/`.
- When an article and an owner doc differ, the owner doc wins.
